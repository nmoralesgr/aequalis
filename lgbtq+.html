LGBTQ+

The LGBTQ+ Community (also known as the LGBT community, GLBT community, gay community, queer community) is a loosely defined grouping of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer individuals. This group is united by shared culture and social movements. These communites celebrate pride, indivuality, diversity, and sexuality. This group often advocates for equal rights and societal change towards queer acceptance, liberation, and rights. This group faces many challenges in the United States.
